The Lodgers in the Hotel

Clara and Jenna are best friends, even though they are actually sisters. When Clara gets an emergency call from her work, at NASA, she has to make a desperate decision, save herself, or throw her coworkers under the bus. Meanwhile, Jenna is working for a fashion designer, when one day she gets a fill-in, who happens to be an alien himself. Will Clara save herself? Will the aliens be civilized? Find out, in the Lodgers in the Hotel.
